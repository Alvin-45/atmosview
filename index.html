February 2024 Weather Forecast: Warmer Nights and Rainier Days In Store for India This Month, IMD Predicts

The ushering of February signifies the gradual transition from the frosty grips of winter to the budding whispers of spring. While typically characterised by chilly winds interspersed with sporadic rain and snow, February 2024 may chart its own course, offering surprises beyond its 29-day span.
Across the Indian subcontinent, hotter-than-usual days await most parts of Northwest, West-Central and Northeast India, along with some areas of East-Central India. Conversely, the maximum temperatures are poised to hover around normal to below-normal levels across most of South Peninsular India, some portions of East-Central India and isolated pockets of Northeast India.

Tamil Nadu To Launch AI-Based Crop Specific Weather Forecast

Tamil Nadu’s Agricuultural University is the first in India to develop weather-based automated agro advisories with zero human intervention.


The Tamil Nadu Agricultural University (TNAU) is all set to launch an Artificial Intelligence (AI)-based crop-specific weather forecast for farmers to reduce the crop failure risk. TNAU is the first in India to develop weather-based automated agro advisories with zero human intervention.“We intend extend this service to eight lakh farmers this year. Location, time and crop-specific micro-level advisory will help farmers reduce the risk of crop failure and harvest the potential from the crop. We will provide crop management, plant protection-related advisories and extreme weather events through the app,” another source with the TNAU said.
